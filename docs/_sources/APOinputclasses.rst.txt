The ``APOinputclasses`` Module
===============================
``APOExptime`` really has one module that take cares of all the necessary calculations to determine the exposure time
needed to reach a desired signal to noise ratio. The ``APOinputclasses`` is primarily composed of three input classes:
``APOinputclasses.Sky``, ``APOinputclasses.Target``, and ``APOinputclasses.Instrument``. Let's take a look at the ``Sky``
class.

The inputs module contains all of the classes and functions you need to calculate an exposure time. When you import
``APOExptime``, it automatically imports the ``inputs`` module. In this manner, you only need to call ``inputs.class``
when using the module. Let's take a look at the ``Instrument`` class.
